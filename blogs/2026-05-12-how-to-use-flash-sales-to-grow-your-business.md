Sometimes a quick revenue surge can make a big difference for a small business—and flash sales can be a helpful tool to bring on this boost. These limited-time offers can drum up excitement, increase conversion and engagement, clear out end-of-season inventory, and more. When done well, flash sales can be a powerful promotional tactic for entrepreneurs looking to grow their business, promote their offerings, and increase sales and profits.
