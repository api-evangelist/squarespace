Squarespace has launched a new Payment Plans feature that lets businesses offer installment-based pricing to customers, helping reduce purchase friction and increase booking conversion rates. The feature integrates directly with Squarespace scheduling and commerce tools, enabling service providers and product sellers to offer flexible payment options without third-party tools.
