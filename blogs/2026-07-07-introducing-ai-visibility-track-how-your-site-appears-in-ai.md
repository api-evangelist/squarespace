AI search is adding a new layer to how people find information and businesses online. Getting your website on the first page of search results used to be one of the best ways to get in front of your intended audience. But as AI summaries and chats become more commonplace, more people are starting their searches directly in AI chat and getting direct responses without leaving the platform.
