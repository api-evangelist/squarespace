Home services marketing works best when it answers one question: “Can I trust you in my space?” Yes, a customer will look at pricing, but they’re also looking for signals that you’re reliable, experienced, and easy to work with. Your marketing should reassure them and reinforce that trust. That means showing up in the right places, backing up your claims with social proof, and staying in contact long after the job is done.
