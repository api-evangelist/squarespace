Hiring a web designer is a big decision, and it’s about much more than just how your site looks. Your website is your digital storefront, a key sales tool, and often the first impression people have of your brand . If it’s your first time hiring a designer, you’ll likely have lots of questions around budget, timelines, communication, and control.
